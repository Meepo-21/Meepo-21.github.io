The Gazo

The Gazo is one of many valuable adversaries to have on your team. Being equipped with a gazo pistol,gazo rifle, and paintball gun to fend off many a enphoso. Having many ranged tools at its disposal. While lacking close ranged weaponry,it makes up for it with sheer firepower. With many optional weapons early into your defense.It may go unnoticed but its value is tremendous. With the gazo rifle able to be a saving grace in tight decisions

As long as you have a Gazo no enphosi will dare get close to the statue and harm it. The gazo rifle is the pinnacle of direct ranged capabilities. Being a solid choice of weaponry for all species regardless of equipment.For if no choice can be made the Gazo is the old reliable.

The Glaaber

Without a doubt one of the most important to have on a team. Unfortunately they aren't as versatile as a Gazo. Mainly due to their role. With a Glaaber club,slow but with amazing power.A pumpkin launcher with explosions,allowing for a Glaaber to deal with multiple a enphoso in short time Lastly,a hungry ham. A weapon of unknown origin to others but contains a power great for Glaabers. Hits with this heals the wielder.

The Glaaber is at the fore front of every fight. Allthough slow,its sheer damage output allows you to ignore it. Others are more self sufficient but the Glaaber requires further assistance so it won't die immedietaly. As long as you keep your Glaaber alive and well? Victory is in your hands and the rest of GlaggleLand with these halloween lovers.

The Gleeber

The Gleeber is a heavily mobile species of Glaggle. They are more calm and collected,being harder to anger then most Glaggle species. Their attiude is heavily reflected in their equipment. A skateboard,the most used or bought equipment from other species then any other equipment native to other species. But for good reason. The skateboard allows for amazing knockback capabilities to push enphoso away. Along with skating on it for extraordinary speed and knockback upon collision. They also come with a gleebox,knocking you and enemies up into the air while pushing enemies away. Not particularily useful but still has uses. Last but not least is the juice blaster. Speeding anyone including yourself with burst of speed. Glaggonian and enphoso alike. A common buy for Glaabers

The gleeber has very weak damage output but its equipment is amazing for any and all species of glaggle. The Gleeber will be flying through the battlefield with insane speeds and assistance to others. Weak but important to all as one of the backbones of every battalion

The Gloober

The Gloober is possibly the weakest but most important in combat but also the second backbone in every battalion. The Gloober is the only other species with the equipment to heal others. Through the Gloober sign healing everyone in a small radius. The Goobab gun which damages enemies and heals teammates. Finally, the Gloobnut which heals your teammates.

The importance of the gloober is not to be underestimated. Although medpads can be found through out your battle field,only with the Gloober do you not have to travel or wait. The Gloobers are what keep you and your team alive against enphosos. Through Gloobers we can keep or morale and stay steady in this war against the enphosian forces. For you are one of the many soldiers of Glaggleland. But also one of the most importance.
